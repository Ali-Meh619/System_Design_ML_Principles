# Interview Day Playbook

## 10 Minutes Before

- Pick one default stack in your head (API, DB, cache, queue).
- Prepare your requirement questions and estimation template.
- Decide your diagram style and notation.

## During Interview

1. Clarify and restate scope.
2. Estimate scale and mark peak traffic.
3. Draw baseline architecture fast.
4. Prioritize one deep dive path.
5. Discuss failures and observability.
6. Close with trade-offs and future evolution.

## If You Get Stuck

- Return to requirements.
- Reduce scope to one critical user flow.
- State assumptions and proceed.

## Closing Template

"This design is optimized for __. The major risk is __. I mitigate it using __. If constraints shift toward __, I would replace __ with __."
