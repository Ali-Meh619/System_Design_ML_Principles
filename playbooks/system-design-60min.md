# 60-Minute System Design Round

## Time Box

- 0-10 min: requirement discovery and scope
- 10-18 min: estimations and SLO targets
- 18-32 min: high-level architecture
- 32-50 min: two deep dives
- 50-60 min: reliability, security, cost, and roadmap

## Deep Dive Menu

Pick two:

- data model and partitioning
- consistency and transaction strategy
- cache invalidation and hot-key handling
- queueing semantics and retries
- multi-region failover
